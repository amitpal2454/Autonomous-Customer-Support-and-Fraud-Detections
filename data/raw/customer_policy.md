# Customer Support & Return Policy

## 1. Overview
At ShopEase, we are committed to providing a seamless customer experience. This document outlines our policies regarding returns, refunds, exchanges, and customer support.

---

## 2. Return Policy

### 2.1 Eligibility
Customers can request a return if:
- The product is damaged, defective, or incorrect
- The return request is raised within 7 days of delivery

### 2.2 Conditions
- Product must be unused and in original packaging
- All accessories and invoices must be included

### 2.3 Non-Returnable Items
- Perishable goods
- Personal care products
- Items marked as “non-returnable”

---

## 3. Refund Policy

### 3.1 Refund Process
- Refunds are initiated after product inspection
- It may take 5–7 business days for processing

### 3.2 Refund Methods
- Original payment method
- Store credits (optional)

### 3.3 Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be issued if:
- Product is used or damaged by the customer
- Missing components

---

## 4. Exchange Policy

- Exchanges are allowed for size or defective products
- Exchange requests must be placed within 7 days
- Replacement is subject to stock availability

---

## 5. Order Cancellation

### 5.1 Before Shipment
- Orders can be canceled fully

### 5.2 After Shipment
- Cancellation is not allowed
- Customer must initiate a return instead

---

## 6. Fraud Prevention Policy

We monitor suspicious activities such as:
- Frequent return requests
- High-value refunds
- Multiple accounts with similar details

### 6.1 Actions Taken
- Account may be flagged
- Refund requests may be denied
- Additional verification may be required
